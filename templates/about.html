Overview

The Department of Physics was established in 1960 and has grown into a center of excellence for research and education in fundamental and applied physics.

Our Vision

To be a leading department in physics education and research, fostering innovation, curiosity, and scientific excellence.

Our Mission

  • Promote research in emerging areas of physics.
  • Equip students with strong theoretical and practical foundations.
  • Engage with national and international scientific communities.
